PRIVACY POLICY

★ ALI'S RETRO ARCADE ★
Last updated: 19 May 2026

Short version: Ali's Retro Arcade games — including FighterJet Retro — collect no personal data. The games run entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to a server. There is no account, no login, no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

What data we collect

None. Our apps do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information, device identifiers, location data, contacts, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics.

What is stored on your device

The games store some information locally on your iPhone so they can function as expected:

  • Game progress (which levels you've completed)
  • High scores
  • Preferences (music on/off, sound effects on/off)

This data is stored using iOS UserDefaults. It never leaves your device, and we have no way of accessing it. If you delete the app, iOS deletes all of this data with it.

Third parties

We do not embed any third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising networks, crash reporters, or tracking libraries. No third parties receive data from these apps because the apps do not send data to anyone.

Network usage

The games do not require an internet connection and do not make network requests. They will function identically in airplane mode.

Children's privacy

Because the games collect no data of any kind, they comply by design with COPPA, GDPR, and similar privacy regulations covering children. They are rated 9+ on the App Store.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if a future game adds optional features that touch the network), we will update this page and the App Store description before the change takes effect. Apps in the App Store today, including FighterJet Retro, are bound by this version of the policy.

Contact
