Common questions

How do I save my progress?
All games save progress automatically. Look for a Load button on the main menu to resume.
How do I turn off the music or sound effects?
Open the Audio menu from the main screen. Music and sound effects can be toggled independently, with separate volume controls.
Do the games work offline?
Yes — completely. No internet connection is required at any point.
Do the games collect any of my data?
No. We collect no personal information. Game data (high scores, settings, progress) is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. Nothing leaves your device.
How do I delete my saved data?
Uninstall the game — iOS will delete all local data with it.
Which iPhone models are supported?
iPhone X and newer, running iOS 14 or later. Games are portrait-only and designed for one-thumb play.
Will there be more games / updates?
Yes — new games and updates are planned. Watch this page or the App Store for new releases.
